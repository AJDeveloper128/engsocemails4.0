ESW: Project Updates & Meetings

From building hydroponic systems to developing recycled filament and starting construction on our composter, there’s lots of progress underway for Engineers for a Sustainable World (ESW)! Here’s what our teams have been working on:

🥬 Feed the Future (Hydroponics): The first header section has been assembled, with the remaining sections underway. The water line is now set up to pump from the in-ground tank through a reservoir and out to all four sections.

🔁 Plastic for Parts (Polyformer): Work continues on filament fusion, and the team has started building a filament winder while printing parts and sourcing electronics. Recent meetings have highlighted skill-building on 3D printing and Arduino/ESP32 coding.

🌱 Scraps to Soil (Composter): With materials purchased, the team has started measurements and the first cuts for the stand. Next steps include assembling the inner frame.

🌿 Gryph’s Gardens (Sustainability App): With their vision set, GG has launched a preliminary demo, added a working camera system, and started building the virtual garden world, all while locking in their Minimum Viable Product (MVP) goals.

Interested in What GryphDex Looks Like?

Hi!! I'm Riri, co-president of GryphDEX. I started my computer engineering degree in 2019 and worked alongside my degree for 3 years full-time to make sure it was feasible. What my work-experience showed me from grinding my way up the corporate ladder was that Guelph Engineering didn't provide me the multi-team organization experience necessary to excel in the workplace.

Thus, I've gradually grown GryphDEX into what you see before you! As you can see, our project teams are fully supported by our executive teams. That way, team communication and collaboration is fostered. A great example would be say for when our developers need to wireframe out a website - the lovely graphic designers help us get it done!

Need something from our execs? We built pathways to make it easier. Want to join an atmosphere that really cares about collaboration? Join GryphDEX!! The Gryphon Design and Engineering Exchange. It's the perfect work atmosphere for a co-op less degree.
