Town Hall Changes

ATTENTION ENGINEERS: Town Hall is changing! With the expansion of the College of Engineering, we are improving how we take your feedback. Previously, one Academic Town Hall would be hosted per semester. We are changing to have a department-specific Town Hall for EACH DEPARTMENT, resulting in a total of FOUR Town Hall events. With this change, your course feedback is given directly to your respective Department Head, bringing you immediate experience-driven responses.

The Departments & Chairs are as follows:
• Water, Civil, and Environmental Eng. - Dr. Andy Binns
• Mechanical Eng. - Dr. Marwan Hassan
• Biomedical, Biological, Mechatronics (Interdisciplinary) - Dr. Hari Simha
• Computer & ESC - Dr. Ahmed Hussein

This change aims to provide you with faster, more accurate answers for your questions and ensure that your feedback is given to the best-suited recipient.
