CoCreCo Exec & Sub-team Applications

Want project experience? Leadership roles?
Figure out what industries or technologies you enjoy, and how you work in real team based roles. Have fun, join a community, and gain career defining experience. That is CoCreCo.

We support all engineering majors and adjacent programs, with multi disciplinary teams.

What does CoCreCo do?

  • Textbook rental program at affordable prices.
  • Guelph Engineering Competition, GEC2025, with the School of Engineering and EngSoc.
  • Project Teams across disciplines:
    • Developers, database and cloud, website, and machine learning, using a 20,000 dollar ML server on campus.
    • Mechanical, automated textbook rental lockers.
    • Biomedical, human computer interfacing methods.
    • Graphic Design, portfolio building work for marketing and competitions.

Want to start a new sub team, for example Environmental, Biological, or Water Resources Engineering, reach out to @ricbq on Instagram.
