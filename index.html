Scott Robertson Award Nominations

This is the 30th anniversary of the Scott Robertson Award, given to the graduating student who most embodies Guelph engineering and goes above-and-beyond for the College. We are seeking nominations from students across all years for the person they feel most captures the spirit of the award. There is no academic component. This individual is highly respected, has created positive impact for the College over the years, and contributed exceptionally to the spirit we associate with engineering at this University. This is the person you think of when you think of Guelph engineering.

Please fill out the nomination form for people you deem suitable for the award. That allows the past winners (36 in total stretching back to 1997) to deliberate on new winner(s) for 2026 ‚Äì a milestone year for the award.

IEEE Battle Royale (Gaming for Charity)

Join the IEEE Student Branch at the University of Guelph (and students across Canada) for the annual IEEE Battle Royale! This is a multi-day event where students can participate in gaming competitions and game jams for charity.

Tournaments will be streamed on Twitch, and all proceeds will be donated directly to UNICEF. Participants in games like Valorant, League of Legends, Minecraft, Fortnite, Chess, and Clash Royale have a chance to win cash prizes!
